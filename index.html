Research

Terahertz Devices and Intersubband Nanostructures Group

As a part of E87, an introductory engineering course, I was fortunate to take part in research as a freshman for a quarter. The research involved measuring electrical resistivity and contact resistance of thin film materials. The task for me and my lab mates was to first set up the probing station where the measurements were to take place. After we needed to create a program in LabView that set up all the instruments to gather our data.

With the setup of the station and program we were able to take varying measurements by adjusting the distance of the two probe needles and by sending current through the material. Using the data gathered along with some calculations and graph we were able to find the resistance of the materials.
